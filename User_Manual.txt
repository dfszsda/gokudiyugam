Gokudiyugam App - User Manual (v1.9.2)
======================================

1. Introduction:
   Gokudiyugam is the official digital companion for the BAPS Swaminarayan Mandal, Badalpur. This app provides devotees with seamless access to daily spiritual content and offers administrators powerful tools for mandal management.

2. Getting Started:
   - Account Creation: Sign up with your email and verify it to unlock all features.
   - Profile Setup: Personalize your profile with your name, contact details, and mandal affiliation.
   - Language Preferences: Choose between English, Gujarati, and Hindi. You can switch languages anytime from the Settings or the Splash screen.

3. Devotional Features:
   - Home Dashboard: View the latest announcements and featured events through the dynamic image slider.
   - Daily Darshan: Access high-resolution daily darshan photos from Badalpur Mandir.
   - Kirtan & Media:
     - Listen to a vast library of devotional kirtans with a professional background media player.
     - Search for kirtan lyrics and create your own playlists.
   - Video Gallery: Watch spiritual videos and discourses directly via the integrated YouTube player.
   - Sabha Timetable: Check the schedules for Yuva, Bal, Mahila, and other sabhas, including topics and guest speakers.
   - Satsang News & Newsletters: Stay updated with the latest happenings and "Sabha Saar" summaries.

4. Admin & Host Features:
   - Admin Panel: A dedicated hub for managing app content and user access.
   - Advanced User Monitoring: Track user activity, including "Online" status, last seen timestamps, and login device details.
   - Role & Permission Management: Assign roles (Host, Sub-Host, Normal) and grant specific screen-level permissions to team members.
   - Home Slider Control: Upload, update, or remove banner images on the home screen in real-time.
   - AI-Powered Translation: Admins can use the "Translate (Lipi)" button to automatically generate Gujarati and Hindi content for sabha topics and news.
   - Help & Feedback: View and manage feedback submitted by devotees to improve mandal services.

5. Security & Privacy:
   - Biometric Authentication: Enable Fingerprint or Face ID in settings for secure and instant access.
   - Content Safety: All user-generated content is monitored using Google Gemini AI to maintain a respectful spiritual environment.
   - Reliable Data Sync: Your profile and role data are securely synchronized with administrative Google Sheets for official record-keeping.
